Director's Message

Aroma School, the Educational Institution run by the Lokhandwala Charitable Trust was established in 1980. Initially began as a Primary school, it gives me immense please to mention that it developed into a Higher Secondary School(English & Gujarati Medium) catered to the educational needs of the people in and around USmanpura and other localities of Ahmedabad City for more than three and a half decades. It is joyful to note that many of our students have achieved glittering success in their life and career. They truly are spreading the Aroma of our institution in several parts of our country and abroad.

Presently the Lokhandwala Trust is running a full fledged Commerce College in the same campus. I have the full contentment to state that the institution has rendered a noble and noteworthy social service to the society with wholehearted co-operation and support of all the staff and other well-wishers. I express my sincere gratitude and extend the best wishes to all.
