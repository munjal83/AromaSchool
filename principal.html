From the Principal's Desk

Our institution from its humble beginning in 1980 flowered into a Higher Secondary School (Gujarati & English Medium) has served the society of Usmanpura & nearby areas of Ahmedabad in meeting the basic educational needs. It gives me joy to note that our school's alumni are serving the society in various levels & capacities. many having a prosperous & glowing career & life.

I have received the full co-operation and support of all staff to achieve the aim, growth and success of the institution. I am grateful to all and it gives me pleasure and full satisfaction that the institution has rendered the noble service, imparting knowledge to large number of students for more than 35 years, many of them spread the AROMA of the Aroma Parivar not only in Gujarat but many places in India and even abroad.

I hereby express my sincere gratefullness to you all. May God Bless Us
